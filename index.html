Bottom line

If the gorillas, golden monkeys, and keeping the spirit of your original route are the priority, the updated TourCan quote is still the best fit. It is no longer as far above budget as the first round, but it does still sit above the ideal range.

If staying below the $40k ceiling matters more than keeping Rwanda, the Goway Fairmont option is the strongest middle ground. It preserves a premium feel with better lodge character than the lower-cost Kenya circuit.

If price leads every decision, the Wonders of Kenya option works — but it should be viewed as a different trip rather than a trimmed version of the original.
